Breathing new life into waste paper.

We use entirely discarded paper to create paper goods that make sure people aren't restraining themselves when choosing to be kind to the environment.

We hope to inspire the school community to view waste through a new perspective.

How to "purchase" our free products

Come to our self-serve shop in Bryan Lobby!

Different items are marked with "price tags" indicating how many sheets of paper were used to make the product.

Simply add at least that number of sheets to the Incoming Paper bin to "pay" for your "purchase".

Paper you add to the Incoming Paper bin should be used paper that still has a blank side.
